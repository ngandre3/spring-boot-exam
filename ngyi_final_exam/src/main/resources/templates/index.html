Try to guess the name of a movie!


Game Rules

A movie will be randomly selected from a list. The movies are famous G-rated Disney and Pixar animated movies.

You will be asked to guess a letter (a-z). If your letter was found in the movie name, then you have a chance to guess the whole movie name.

Winning the Game: If you guess the movie name correctly, then you win the game.

Losing the Game: If you guess the letter or movie name wrongly, then you earn 1 fail. If you get 7 fails, you lose the game.

Scoring: A game ends after 7 fails or when the movie name is guessed correctly. You gain 5 points when you win a game. You lose 5 points when you lose a game.
